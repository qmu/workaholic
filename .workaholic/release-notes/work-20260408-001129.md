# Introduce four-tier lead skill structure etc

## Summary

Matured the lead skill architecture with a four-tier knowledge framework (Role, Policies, Practices, Standards), consolidated 10 lead domains to 7, fixed the drive Abandon flow to stop the session, and added dependency tracking to ticket frontmatter for ordered execution of split tickets.

## Key Changes

- Introduced four-tier lead skill structure with schema enforcement, validation checklist, and example content
- Consolidated 10 leads into 7 by merging infra+recovery into reliability and a11y into ux
- Added `depends_on` frontmatter field enabling topological sorting of split ticket execution order

## Changes

### Added

- Practices and Standards sections to lead schema template, guidelines, validation, and example
- Practices and Standards headings to all 7 lead skills
- Practices content for quality and ux lead skills
- AI e2e testability policy to quality lead and contrast/motion standard to ux lead
- `depends_on` optional field to ticket frontmatter schema with validation hook and drive-navigator topological sorting
- Lead skill preloading into architect, constructor, planner, and ticket-organizer agents
- Domain-specific principles for security, db, infrastructure, and recovery leads

### Changed

- Drive Abandon option now stops the session instead of silently continuing to next ticket
- Lead count reduced from 10 to 7 via domain consolidation
- Lead agent, manager skills, and scan agent selection updated for 7-domain set

### Removed

- lead-infra, lead-recovery, lead-a11y, and lead-test skill files (content merged into reliability and ux)
