# Attempt-first night mode, OKF compatibility, and carry Findings/Decisions

## Summary

Three workflow constraints are tightened: night-mode `/drive` becomes attempt-first, the generated knowledge tree becomes Open Knowledge Format (OKF v0.1) compatible, and the `/carry` resumption ticket now preserves the session's reasoning alongside its remaining tasks.

## Key Changes

- Night mode must attempt every authorized ticket; a skip is legitimate only on a demonstrated failure or a named hard external blocker, never on size, complexity, or "needs a human"
- The four pillars' policies ship as a committed OKF v0.1 bundle, and every project's `.workaholic/` tree is kept OKF-compatible as workflows generate documents, refreshed by a new internal `okf` skill
- The `/carry` resumption ticket gains optional `## Findings` and `## Decisions` sections so a fresh `/drive` neither re-walks a ruled-out dead-end nor relitigates a settled choice

## Changes

### Added
- OKF v0.1 export bundle (`outputs/okf/`) and runtime `.workaholic/` OKF compatibility, with a self-maintaining index hierarchy
- `## Findings` and `## Decisions` sections in the `/carry` resumption ticket template

### Changed
- Night-mode `/drive` skip policy: attempt-first, with a closed-set morning report of implemented / failed / blocked
