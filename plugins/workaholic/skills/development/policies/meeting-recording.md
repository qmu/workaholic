# Transcribing Client Meetings

_Recording client meetings with prior consent and converting them to searchable transcripts, so that the decision-making process is preserved as a shared record for both client and team._

We record our client meetings with prior consent and use AI to transcribe and summarize the audio, organizing it into a state that can be searched and referenced later.

## Goal (目標)

We aim for a state in which both the client and our side can return to the same record afterward. The discussions in a project carry a decision-making process: what options were raised, why a particular proposal was set aside, which one was chosen, and which perspectives the client weighted heavily. What remains in code and commit messages is the conclusion that was ultimately adopted. The examination process tends to become difficult to trace as half a year or a year passes, even though it is fresh in memory right after the meeting ends. By recording meetings, we aim for a state in which what was discussed, how each perspective was handled, and why that conclusion was reached can be retrieved from the same record — whether the client is looking back or our side is confirming the basis for a judgment.

## Responsibility (責務)

Our responsibility is to prevent recording data from being used beyond the scope agreed with the client, and to prevent the rationale behind project decisions from being lost to memory alone. "Said / didn't say" becoming an issue is an effect that results as well, but that is not our primary concern. The purpose of recording is to leave the decision-making process that client and our side built together as a shared record.

## Practices (実践)

### Always obtain prior consent

In recording, we always obtain the client's consent in advance. In most cases this is confirmed together with the "pre-project agreement" in [Active Use of Generative AI](ai-utilization.md) — confirming how far the client's information may be handled by AI.

### Handle recording data according to safety policy

The storage, scope of use, and protection handling of recording data are operated in accordance with our safety policy. Rather than repeating specific rules here, we refer to the safety policy side as the central record:

- [Information Security Policy](../../safety/policies/policy.md)
- [Privacy Policy](../../safety/policies/privacy-policy.md)
- [Security Standards](../../safety/policies/standard.md)
- [Incident Reporting Procedure](../../safety/policies/incident-report.md)

Summaries and excerpts obtained from recordings are not used beyond the scope agreed with the client.
