# Information Security Policy

_The company's statement of commitment to protecting information assets through management-led, continuous improvement of information security._

Qmu Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company") protects the information assets of the Company and the information assets entrusted to us by our clients from threats such as accidents, disasters, and crime, and works on information security across the entire company based on the following policy in order to meet the trust of our clients and society.

## Goal (目標)

We aim for a state in which the Company's information assets and those entrusted to us by clients are protected from threats such as accidents, disasters, and crime, and in which the Company continuously improves information security under management leadership. We aim for a state in which all employees have acquired the knowledge and skills necessary for information security, and in which internal rules and organizational structure for maintaining and improving information security are in place and functioning.

## Responsibility (責務)

Our responsibility is to prevent a state in which information security governance is left to individual effort rather than driven by management, or in which applicable laws, regulations, norms, and contractual obligations are not met. We prevent a state in which breaches and incidents are not responded to appropriately, or in which recurrence is not prevented after an incident.

## Practices (実践)

### 1. Management responsibility

The Company strives for organizational and continuous improvement and enhancement of information security under management leadership.

### 2. Internal structure

The Company establishes an organization for maintaining and improving information security, and defines information security measures as official internal rules.

### 3. Employee commitment

The Company's employees acquire the knowledge and skills necessary for information security and make their work on information security reliable.

### 4. Compliance with laws and contractual requirements

The Company complies with laws, regulations, norms, and contractual obligations related to information security, and meets the expectations of clients.

### 5. Response to violations and incidents

The Company responds appropriately when violations of laws, breach of contracts, or incidents related to information security occur, and works to prevent recurrence.
