# Privacy Policy

_The company's commitment to collecting, using, and protecting personal information only to the extent necessary for stated business purposes and in accordance with applicable law._

Qmu Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company") recognizes that it is a social responsibility to handle the personal information of clients and stakeholders appropriately, and works to protect personal information based on the following policy.

## Goal (目標)

We aim for a state in which personal information is obtained only within the scope necessary for business operations through lawful and fair means, used only within the stated purposes, and protected through organizational, human, physical, and technical safety management measures. We aim for a state in which individuals' requests for disclosure, correction, or suspension of use are responded to appropriately and promptly in accordance with applicable law, and in which the Company continuously reviews and improves its approach to personal information protection.

## Responsibility (責務)

Our responsibility is to prevent a state in which personal information is used beyond the stated purpose without the individual's consent, shared with third parties without consent, or handled by contractors without appropriate supervision. We prevent a state in which the Company's approach to personal information falls out of compliance with applicable law and guidelines, or in which requests from individuals for disclosure, correction, or suspension of use are not handled appropriately.

## Practices (実践)

### Obtaining personal information

The Company obtains personal information only within the scope necessary for business operations through lawful and fair means. In obtaining information, the Company states the purpose of use and, in principle, does so with the consent of the individual.

### Purposes of use

The Company uses obtained personal information within the scope of the following purposes:

- Responding to inquiries from clients
- Providing services based on outsourcing contracts and carrying out related business
- Information about the Company's services
- Recruitment screening and recruitment activities
- Responses based on applicable law

When personal information is handled beyond the scope of the stated purposes, the Company obtains the individual's consent in advance.

### Restrictions on provision to third parties

The Company does not provide personal information to third parties without the individual's consent, except in the following cases:

- When required by law
- When necessary for the protection of the life, body, or property of a person and it is difficult to obtain the individual's consent
- When specially necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound development of children and it is difficult to obtain the individual's consent
- When it is necessary to cooperate with a national institution, local government, or their contractor in executing duties prescribed by law, and obtaining the individual's consent is likely to impede execution of those duties

### Safety management measures

The Company implements organizational, human, physical, and technical safety management measures to prevent unauthorized access to personal information, loss, destruction, falsification, and leakage. Specific measures are implemented in accordance with the Company's [Security Standards](standard.md).

### Supervision of contractors

When the Company outsources handling of personal information, it selects contractors that have implemented sufficient safety management measures and exercises appropriate supervision through contracts and other means.

### Requests for disclosure, correction, suspension of use, etc.

When a request for disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use, or suspension of provision to third parties of personal information is received from the individual, the Company responds within a reasonable period and scope in accordance with applicable law. Please contact the following for inquiries.

### Compliance with laws and continuous improvement

The Company complies with laws relating to the protection of personal information, guidelines established by the government, and other norms, and continuously reviews and works to improve the content of this policy.
